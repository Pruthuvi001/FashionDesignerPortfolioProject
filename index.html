About Me

Some Informations about me

Who Am I?

I am Hewa Walasmullage Vishwanee Divani , born and raised in Sri Lanka by amazing parents who have always recognized the value of a great education and inspired me to excel in all my pursuits.

What I Do?

As an individual, I am diligent, inquisitive, independent, enthusiastic and highly motivated. Further, I love learning and am a student who constantly strives to pursue my passions and achieve ambitious goals.

Why Fashion?

In my point of view, I believe that fashion is a mirror which reflects the inner personality of a person.self expression is difficult to convey where I want people to express themselves through fashion and this is the main reason of selecting fashion design as my career

Do I Can?

I have gained Knowledge on fashion design, illustrations, fashion history, trend forecasting, pattern making and Computer Aided Design (CAD).
